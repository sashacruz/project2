Animal Poaching

Animal poaching has been a very big problem since it started back around 1900 when people from europe came and started to hunt big game for fun. After that the "sport" became a huge problem. There are some reasons why people poach, They could be hunting for commercial profit like for example the tusks of an elephant are used to make piano keys, identification chops and many other items. Some people hunt for the fun of it and that's why some people have animal heads as decoration on their walls. This selfish practice of killing animals for money is horrible and it's hurting the environment and it puts people in danger and it needs to stop.
